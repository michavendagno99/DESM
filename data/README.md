# Data

The raw and merged datasets used in this study (World Bank World Development
Indicators, Worldwide Governance Indicators, the QoG Standard dataset,
Barro-Lee educational attainment, CEPII gravity/trade data, and the Global
Financial Development Database) are **not distributed in this repository**
— they total several hundred megabytes across source files, most of which
are third-party redistributions with their own licensing terms.

What is included:

- [`sources.txt`](sources.txt) — a source-by-source provenance note for
  every dataset used in the institutional-coupling (`γ`) estimation,
  including what each dataset contains and where it comes from.

All datasets referenced are public and can be retrieved directly from their
original providers (World Bank, Quality of Government Institute, Barro-Lee,
CEPII). For the exact merged panel used to produce the article's
estimates, tables, and figures, contact the author (see
[`../README.md`](../README.md)).
